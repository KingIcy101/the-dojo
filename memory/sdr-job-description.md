## Job Title
**Remote Cold Outreach Specialist – Healthcare Marketing Agency (Colombia Preferred)**

---

## About Us

Halo Marketing is a fast-growing marketing agency exclusively serving healthcare practitioners — chiropractors, dentists, telehealth providers, and specialty clinics. We help them get more patients through Facebook and Google ads, automated follow-up systems, and hands-on account management.

We're a small, ambitious team based in Maryland and we're growing fast. We're looking for our next key hire to help us fill our sales pipeline with qualified discovery calls.

---

## The Role

You'll be the first point of contact for potential clients. Your job: reach out to healthcare practitioners via cold calls and cold email, spark genuine conversations, and book discovery calls with our founders.

This is a pure outreach role — no closing, no account management. Just consistent, professional outreach and appointment setting.

**What you'll do:**
- Make 50–80 cold calls per day to healthcare practices across the US
- Send personalized cold emails using our sequences (Saleshandy)
- Qualify leads and book discovery calls on our calendar
- Track all activity in our CRM (GoHighLevel)
- Follow up consistently — most calls take 3–7 touches
- Report daily on calls made, emails sent, and calls booked

---

## Who You Are

- **You speak excellent English** — phone-clear, confident, natural. You can hold a professional conversation with a doctor's office receptionist without hesitation.
- **You're persistent and thick-skinned** — rejection is daily. You reset fast and keep dialing.
- **You're reliable and self-directed** — you show up to work at the same time every day and hit your targets without someone hovering.
- **You care about quality** — a booked call with the wrong fit is worse than no call. You pre-qualify.
- **You've done outreach before** — some experience in cold calling, appointment setting, or sales is required. Healthcare is a plus, not a must.
