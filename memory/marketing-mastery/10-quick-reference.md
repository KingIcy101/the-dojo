## Niche Quick Guide

### Chiropractic
- Ad hook: Pain-specific (back, neck, sciatica, headache)
- Best offer: $49 new patient exam
- Target radius: 5–10 miles
- Best platform: Facebook + Google
- LTV per patient: $1,500–$4,000

### Dental
- Ad hook: Segment-specific (new patient, cosmetic, emergency, family)
- Best offer: Free whitening with new patient, or 0$ cleaning with insurance
- Target radius: 5–15 miles
- Best platform: Facebook (cosmetic/family) + Google (emergency)
- LTV per patient: $2,000–$6,000

### Telehealth
- Ad hook: Convenience + condition-specific
- Best offer: Free first consult, or flat-fee intro visit
- Target: State(s) where licensed; or national
- Best platform: Facebook (awareness) + Google (intent)
- LTV per patient: $2,500–$8,000

## The Patient Machine (Halo's Automation Flow)

```
Lead submits form
  → T+30s: Automated SMS confirmation
  → T+2–5min: Bland.ai call to book
  → If no answer: Follow-up sequence (7 days, 5–7 touches)
  → Appointment booked: Confirmation SMS + email
  → 24hr before: Reminder + confirm
  → 2hr before: Final reminder
  → Post-visit: Review request SMS
  → 30/60/90 days: Reactivation sequence
```

---

## Sales Call Flow

1. **Permission + intent** (2 min): "I want this to be useful either way — I'll ask questions first"
2. **Situation** (3–5 min): Size, services, current volume, how they get patients
3. **Problem** (3–5 min): What's broken, what they've tried, what they wish was different
4. **Implication** (2–3 min): What flat growth costs them
5. **Need-payoff** (2 min): "If we fixed this, what changes?"
6. **Pitch** (5 min): Leads → follow-up → bookings → reporting
7. **Handle objections** → Ask for the close
