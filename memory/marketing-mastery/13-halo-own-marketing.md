## The Core Idea: Eat Your Own Cooking

Every top agency owner says some version of this: **if you're running ads for clients but not for yourself, you're leaving the fastest growth lever untouched.**

Halo's competitive advantage IS digital marketing. Use it to acquire clients faster than cold outreach alone can.

The math is compelling:
- Cold calling: SDR closes 1–2 clients/month at 50 calls/day
- Cold email: maybe 2–4 clients/month with strong sequences
- Running ads TO practitioners: could add 3–8 clients/month at scale
- All three together: 6–14 clients/month = 150 clients in ~12–18 months

Facebook's ad platform doesn't have "chiropractor" as an interest — but you can reach them through:

**Job title targeting:**
- "Chiropractor" / "Chiropractic physician"
- "Dentist" / "Dental surgeon" / "Orthodontist"
- "Physician" + interest filters

**Employer targeting:**
- Small businesses + healthcare industry + specific practice name keywords
- Job title = owner, partner, practice administrator

**Interest-based targeting:**
- Medical / dental / chiropractic professional organizations
- "American Chiropractic Association" / "ADA" audiences
- Healthcare industry professionals

**Behavioral targeting:**
- Small business owners in the healthcare sector
- People who frequently travel for business (CE courses, conferences)

**The "lookalike" shortcut:**
- Upload Halo's current client list (even 2–3 contacts) → create a 1% LAL

- This is powerful: you're telling Facebook "find me people like these healthcare business owners"

## The Lead Magnet Strategy

A lead magnet converts cold ad traffic into warm leads without needing a direct "book a call" ask.

### Best lead magnets for agency B2B:

**1. Free Practice Marketing Audit (highest value, highest conversion)**
- "We'll review your current online presence, ads (if any), and local competition — and give you a specific growth plan."
- Requires a 15-minute intake call → this IS the discovery call, just positioned as an audit
- Conversion: very high (practitioners love free analysis of their specific situation)

**2. "Patient Acquisition Checklist" PDF**
- "The 12-step checklist healthcare practices use to consistently add 20+ new patients/month"
- Low friction (no call needed to get it)
- Builds email list → nurture sequence → discovery call eventually
- Good for top-of-funnel volume

**3. Case Study PDF**
- "How [Practice Type] in [State] Added [X] New Patients in [Timeframe]"
- Specific and credible
- Ideal for warming practitioners who clicked an ad but aren't ready to call

**4. Webinar (Robb Bailey model)**
- 45–60 minute Zoom webinar
- First 40 min: genuine education (the stuff in Halo's marketing playbooks)
- Last 10 min: soft pitch for a discovery call
- Requires $300–$1,000 in ads to fill the webinar
- Converts 5–15% of attendees to discovery calls
- At scale: most efficient client acquisition model for agencies

## Halo's Own Content Strategy (Parallel to Ads)

While ads bring paid traffic, content builds organic authority. Start this in Stage 2 (5+ clients).

### LinkedIn (highest ROI for B2B agency)

Post 3x/week. Rotate through:
1. **Results posts:** "Client result: [Specialty] practice in [City] added [X] new patients in [timeframe]. Here's what we did." (Specifics always outperform vague claims)
2. **Educational posts:** "Why healthcare marketing fails: the 5 mistakes most agencies make." (Position as expert)
3. **Behind the scenes:** "Set up a new campaign for a dental client today. Here's the targeting approach we used..." (Transparency builds trust)
4. **Opinion posts:** "Hot take: if your marketing agency can't show you CPL, booked calls, and show rate — they're not measuring the right things." (Generates debate, builds reputation)
5. **Practitioner pain points:** "Every chiropractor I talk to says the same thing: referrals are great but unreliable. Here's the fix." (Speaks directly to prospects)

**Consistency beats virality.** 3 posts/week for 6 months builds a meaningful audience and inbound pipeline.

### YouTube (longer term, Stage 3)

Channel positioning: "The healthcare practice growth channel — real tactics from a real agency"

**Video ideas with high search volume:**
- "How to get more chiropractic patients from Facebook ads (complete tutorial)"
- "Why your dental marketing isn't working (and what to do instead)"
- "The $49 new patient offer: does it actually work? Our data."
- "Chiropractic patient funnel walkthrough — from ad to booked appointment"
- "GoHighLevel for healthcare practices — complete setup guide"

These videos rank for exact searches practitioners type when they're fed up and looking for solutions.

**The conversion funnel from YouTube:**
- Viewer finds video → watches → goes to description → clicks "Free audit" link → books discovery call → becomes client
