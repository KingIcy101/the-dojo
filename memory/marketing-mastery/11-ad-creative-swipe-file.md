## The Psychology Behind Healthcare Ad Copy

Healthcare patients make decisions differently than regular consumers:
1. **Pain is the primary motivator** — they're not buying a product, they're escaping suffering
2. **Trust threshold is high** — they're letting someone touch their body / give them medicine. Trust matters more than price.
3. **Skepticism is real** — they've tried things before. They don't want another promise that fails.
4. **Speed matters** — when someone's in pain, they want relief now. Urgency is authentic, not manufactured.
5. **Social proof is decisive** — one real patient review beats any claim you make about yourself

Every ad Halo writes should address at least 3 of these 5.

## Video Ad Script Templates

### 60-Second Chiropractic Video (for Facebook/Instagram)

```
[0:00–0:05] HOOK
Show: Person rubbing back/neck in pain
Voiceover or text overlay: "Still dealing with back pain every morning?"

[0:05–0:20] PROBLEM
Voiceover: "Most people try painkillers, heating pads, or 'just living with it.'
But none of those fix what's actually causing the pain."

[0:20–0:40] SOLUTION
Cut to: Clean office, smiling doctor, patient on adjustment table (happy, relaxed)
Voiceover: "At [Practice], we start with a thorough spinal assessment — 
finding exactly where the restriction is causing your pain.
Then we fix it. Not mask it."

[0:40–0:55] PROOF
Cut to: Patient testimonial clip (15 sec, real person)
"I'd had back pain for years. After 8 visits with Dr. [Name], it's gone."

[0:55–1:00] CTA
Text overlay + voiceover: "New patients: $49 exam this month. Tap to book."
```
