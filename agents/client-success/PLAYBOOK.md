## Onboarding Checklist Template

### Day 1
- [ ] Welcome call (Matt or Preston)
- [ ] Collect login access: GHL, ad accounts, existing CRM
- [ ] Send welcome email + Tally intake form
- [ ] PandaDoc contract confirmed signed
- [ ] Payment confirmed received

### Week 1
- [ ] GHL sub-account created for client
- [ ] Landing page built (or existing one reviewed)
- [ ] Ad account connected + pixel installed
- [ ] Bland.ai flow configured (AI appointment reminder)
- [ ] SMS/email automation built in GHL
- [ ] Test lead flow end-to-end

### Week 2
- [ ] First campaign launched (Facebook or Google, depending on niche)
- [ ] Client shown their GHL dashboard
- [ ] First "launch email" sent to client with what to expect

### Day 30 — First Results Review
- [ ] Pull ad metrics: spend, leads, cost per lead
- [ ] Count booked appointments from lead flow
- [ ] Calculate cost per booked appointment
- [ ] Identify what's working + what needs to change
- [ ] Present report on call, get client feedback
- [ ] Set expectations for Month 2

## Retention Risk Indicators
- No check-in in 30+ days → proactive outreach
- Results below baseline for 2+ weeks → flag for Matt
- Client hasn't logged into GHL in 2+ weeks → check in, offer tour
- Complaint or frustration expressed → escalate same day
- Contract renewal within 60 days → start re-commitment conversation now

## Baseline Results by Niche
- Chiro: $15–30 cost per lead, 30–50% booking rate from lead flow
- Dental: $20–40 cost per lead, 25–40% booking rate
- Telehealth: $30–60 cost per lead (higher CPL, but higher LTV)

## Satisfaction Signals for Referral Ask
- Client has been running 60+ days
- Results at or above baseline
- Positive language on check-in calls
- Has recommended Halo organically already (green light for formal ask)

## Escalation Rules
- Escalate to Matt if: client threatens to cancel, ad account suspended, major tech failure, billing dispute
- Escalate to Volt if: results declining for 2+ consecutive weeks
- Never let a client sit unaddressed for more than 24 hours on a complaint
