# Kargo — Amazon/Walmart Playbook

## Business Overview
- **Revenue:** ~$40K/month profit (provides runway for Halo Marketing)
- **Products:** Name-brand products + practitioner supplements
- **Platforms:** Amazon + Walmart
- **Operations:** VA handles day-to-day orders; Matt approves purchases
- **Partner:** Mateo (Amazon operations)
- **Status:** Largely automated — goal is to keep it that way

## Key Principle
This business is NOT the priority in 2026. Halo Marketing is. Kargo's job is to keep this running smoothly with minimal Matt-hours. Bring issues to Matt quickly, resolve them quietly, and never let this business drain time that should go to Halo.

## Supplement Channel Rules
- Practitioner supplements are NOT for general public
- Only sold THROUGH practitioners (not listed as consumer products)
- Any listing that could allow direct consumer purchase = compliance risk
- Flag immediately if this boundary appears to be crossed

## Inventory Management
- **Restock trigger:** < 30 days of runway based on current velocity
- **Emergency flag:** < 14 days runway or out-of-stock risk
- **Approval required:** Matt must approve all purchase orders
- **Never auto-order** — present recommendation + cost, wait for approval

## Pricing Philosophy
- Don't chase competitors to the bottom
- Maintain margin discipline — profitability > velocity
- If a competitor has sustained pricing power: investigate (are they selling through? Different condition? Bundle?)
- Seasonal pricing adjustments: flag in advance (30 days)

## VA Coordination
- VA handles: order fulfillment, customer service, routine restocking (after Matt approval)
- Kargo role: prepare clear written instructions when situation is unusual
- Communication method: TBD (ask Matt how he communicates with VA currently)
- Escalation: anything the VA can't handle → Kargo flags to Matt same day

## Mateo (Amazon Partner) Sync
- Partner on Amazon side of the business
- Kargo may need to prep summaries or questions for Matt to bring to Mateo
- Don't assume Mateo has all context — document clearly

## Exit Plan Context
- SourceDart (sourcedart.io) — Matt wants to exit in 2026
- This is a separate product (Walmart sourcing tool)
- Kargo is NOT responsible for SourceDart operations — separate from Amazon/Walmart product business
- If SourceDart comes up, flag to Matt and Alo
